Wednesday

On Wednesday, I woke up and completed my 142L lab. For lunch, I warmed up a batch of dinosaur chicken nuggets. Around 3 pm a friend from high school and from UCSD came to my house to visit. We hung out and talked about our quarter and our careers. We went to grab 30 wings from Wingstop for dinner. We closed out the night with some frozen yogurt and some Super Smash Bros Ultimate.

Thursday

On Thursday I woke up attend my 9:30 and 11 am class zoom lectures. After these lectures my family and I packed the car and headed to Newport Beach. When we arrived, we walked around found this nice small pizza place called Gina's Pizza. We got back and played some Mario Kart 8 Deluxe on the Nintendo Switch. My mom made us some steak sandwiches for dinner. Afterwards, we entertained ourselves with some Super Smash Bros and some Jackbox party games.


A video of me and some of the family I traveled to Newport Beach with.

Newport Beach

Newport Beach is a coastal city in Orange County, California known for its large habors.

Friday

On Friday, I woke up at 11 am, still at Newport Beach with family and friends. At 11:30am, we left to go to rent a boat to eat lunch and tour the waters of Newport Beach for an hour and a half. After we got back, we play two gigantic games of volleyball on the beach. We washed up and then went to dinner at Bluewater Grill. We finished the night with some ice cream and card games.
